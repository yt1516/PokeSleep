What Am I?

PokéSleep is a non-intrusive IoT device that assigns you your very own Pokémon based on your sleep quality.

How Do I Work?

When a sound is made, the sound sensor records the timestamp of the sound and gets uploaded to a Google Sheet database. Less number of sounds made during the night means better sleep quality. It also records the temperature and humidity of the sleeping environment so you can see how your sleep quality is affected by these factors.



How To Get More Pokémon?

One Pokémon is collected per day, based on the sleep quality of the night. There are legendary Pokémons which have a lower chance of being collected than normal Pokémons. The chance of getting a legendary Pokémon increases permanently if the sleep quality of the night is better than the average, but it can also decrease if it is worse than the average.
